Breed Information

The Golden British Shorthair

Appearance

Golden British Shorthairs are distinguished by their plush, dense coat in various shades of gold, from pale cream to deep copper. They have the classic British round face, chubby cheeks, and sturdy body that make the breed so beloved worldwide.

Temperament

Known for their calm, gentle nature, Golden British cats are affectionate without being demanding. They're excellent companions who enjoy being near their owners while maintaining their independent spirit. Their easygoing temperament makes them perfect for families.

Health & Lifespan

With proper care, Golden British Shorthairs typically enjoy a lifespan of 14-20 years. They are generally robust cats with few breed-specific health concerns. Our breeding program focuses on health testing to ensure the strongest genetic lines.

Health Guaranteed

Health is our top priority. All our breeding cats undergo comprehensive genetic testing, and kittens receive proper veterinary care before joining their new families.

Our Breeding Philosophy

At Golden Elegance Cattery, we are dedicated to breeding exceptional Golden British Shorthairs that exemplify the best qualities of this magnificent breed. Our breeding program is built on three core principles:

  • Health: We conduct comprehensive health testing on all our breeding cats to ensure we pass on only the strongest genetic traits.
  • Temperament: We select breeding cats with exceptional temperaments to produce kittens that are not only beautiful but also make wonderful companions.
  • Type: We strive for cats that meet and exceed breed standards in terms of structure, coat quality, and color.
